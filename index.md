# Privacy Policy — Huijia Finance & Accounts Lab

**Last updated: May 8, 2026**

## Overview

Huijia Finance & Accounts Lab ("the App") is a free, offline educational tool built for Beijing Huijia Private School's IB Business Management students. The App is developed by Matthew Quinn.

## Data Collection

The App does **not** collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. Specifically:

- No account or login is required
- No personal information is requested or collected
- No usage data or analytics are collected
- No cookies or tracking technologies are used
- No data is sent to any server
- No third-party services or SDKs are integrated

## Data Storage

The App stores user-generated content (such as essay responses in the Practice section) locally on the device only, using the device's built-in storage. This data never leaves the device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

## Internet Access

The App operates entirely offline. No internet connection is required or used.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any data from any users, including children under 13. The App is designed for high school students (ages 15–18) studying IB Business Management.

## Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL with an updated date.
